# Task Manager - Strategic Life Organization Tool

## Overview

A comprehensive task management application designed to help you organize and prioritize your entire life across three distinct workspaces: **Work**, **School**, and **Home**. Built with strategic planning in mind, this tool bridges the gap between high-level goals and daily execution.

## Key Features

### 🎯 Multi-Workspace Organization
- Separate your tasks into three dedicated life areas (Work, School, Home)
- Each workspace maintains its own independent hierarchy of domains, strategic pillars, themes, and tasks
- Switch seamlessly between contexts without clutter or confusion

### 📅 Smart Date Prioritization
- **Unique time-span scheduling**: Prioritize a task across multiple days by setting a date range
- Tasks automatically appear on every day within their prioritized period
- No need to manually duplicate tasks or set daily reminders
- Perfect for projects that span several days or weeks

### ⚡ Quick Task Creation
- Rapid task entry with one-click creation menu
- Minimal friction between thought and action
- Create tasks, themes, pillars, and domains on the fly

### 🏗️ Strategic Hierarchy
Organize work using a four-tier strategic framework:
- **Domains**: High-level areas of responsibility
- **Strategic Pillars**: Major initiatives or focus areas within domains
- **Themes**: Specific projects or workstreams
- **Tasks**: Individual action items (with support for subtasks)

### 📊 Multiple Views
- **Today's Priorities**: Focus on what matters most right now
- **Calendar Week View**: Drag-and-drop visual scheduling with daily columns
- **Hierarchy View**: Navigate your strategic structure from top to bottom
- **All Tasks**: Comprehensive list of active tasks grouped by date
- **Completed Tasks**: Track your accomplishments

### 🎨 Visual Task Management
- Color-coded priority levels (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- Status tracking (Open, Hold, Completed)
- Drag-and-drop task reordering and rescheduling
- Theme and status badges for quick identification

### 🔐 Secure & Personal
- User authentication with Supabase
- All data is private and tied to your account
- Row-level security ensures complete data isolation

## Perfect For

- Professionals juggling career, education, and personal life
- Students balancing academics, work, and home responsibilities  
- Anyone seeking a unified system to manage all aspects of life
- Teams or individuals who think strategically about their goals
